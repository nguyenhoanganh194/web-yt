Meet the Team

Lead Developer

Lionel Nguyen

As the lead developer of Cat Cook: Defense, my goal was to create a tower defense game that blends deep strategy with charm and heart. We wanted more than just towers—we wanted chefs, chaos, and creative gameplay. Merge mechanics, customizable loadouts, hero skills, and risk-reward boss events were all carefully tuned to give players exciting choices and satisfying outcomes. We designed every feature to encourage experimentation and empower regardless of your playstyles.

Lead Artist

Minh Dang

As the lead artist of Cat Cook: Defense, I wanted every part of the game to feel like a warm, lively kitchen full of mischief and magic. From the playful animations of our Chef Cats to the bouncy chaos of candy bullets and exploding utensils, every frame is hand-drawn with love. We didn’t just create towers—we created characters, moods, and a world that invites players to smile even in the heat of battle. Our goal was to make every moment on screen as delightful as the gameplay itself.

Designer

Teo Nguyen

As the game designer of Cat Cook: Defense, my mission was to blend depth and accessibility into one irresistible experience. I wanted players to make clever choices—from tower placement and hero timing to risk-reward boss modifiers—without needing a textbook to enjoy it. Every wave, every merge, every kitchen-themed upgrade was crafted to reward creativity, strategy, and just a little bit of chaos. We designed it to be “easy to learn, hard to master”—and impossible to put down.

UA, Growth

Kelly Kieu

Cat Cook: Defense was built from the ground up with long-term engagement and scalable growth in mind. With a rich meta-game of evolving towers, hero loadouts, and gacha-style progression, we give players compelling daily goals and mid-term mastery loops. From early onboarding to competitive endless modes, every system is designed to support retention, monetization, and community-driven replayability. Our unique theme helps us stand out in the crowded tower defense market—and our player data shows they’re staying for more.

Designer

Truong Bui

As a developer on Cat Cook: Defense, I helped bring together fast-paced strategy with charming, kitchen-themed chaos. We wanted players to enjoy deep mechanics—like merging towers, customizing loadouts, and triggering powerful Chef skills—while keeping the game accessible and fun. Watching players experiment with different towers, evolve their items, and overcome enemy waves in creative ways has been incredibly rewarding. It’s a game built to be both delightful and replayable.
